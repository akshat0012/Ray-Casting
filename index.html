2D Ray Casting is an intriguing technique used to simulate the behavior of light rays as they interact with a two-dimensional environment. In the realm of computer graphics and game development, this method serves as a foundational tool for creating realistic lighting effects, shadows, and line-of-sight simulations. One effective way to visualize and comprehend the intricacies of 2D Ray Casting is through the use of the Processing.
